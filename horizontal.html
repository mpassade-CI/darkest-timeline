I Want to Box Amateur Boxing Nationals then Pro Life is Good A Tragic End
2010 2012 2014 2016 2018
Aaron is 18 years old and decides to take up boxing. He signs up in a gym, finds a coach, and begins his training. Aaron has been consistently training for 2 years. He is fairly good and begins competing as an Amateur Boxer. He has 10 fights this year receiving a record of 9-1. He also wins the NY Golden Gloves tournament. This is Aaron's 3rd year competing as an Amateur Boxer. He has 45 fights with a record of 36-9. This year he competes in and wins the biggest National Boxing tournament in the U.S. He then decides to quit the Amateurs and turn Professional. Aaron has seen much success thus far as a Professional Boxer. His professional record stands at 10-0 with 10 KOs. He has been making a lot of money and seems genuinely happy. Aaron is now 17-0 with 16 KOs. He takes on his toughest opponent to date (a guy who's 24-0 with 24 KOs). Aaron fights a good fight and seems to be winning but in the 10th round he gets hit with a punch that knocks him out cold. Aaron loses the fight, but worse than that he never wakes up. He tragically dies 42 hours later in the hospital.
